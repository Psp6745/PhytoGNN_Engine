📖 Biological Compound Classifications

🌿 Terpenoids & Monoterpenes

Highly volatile organic molecules (such as Linalool) known for structural pathway activations. Studies show these compounds can reduce cellular insulin resistance.

🧬 Triterpene Saponins

Heavy compounds like Gymnemic Acids that target glucose absorption in taste receptors and the digestive tract, lowering systemic blood-sugar spikes.

🔋 Polyphenols & Flavonoids

Antioxidant molecules (such as Curcumin) that protect insulin-producing beta-cells from inflammation-induced death, preserving natural pancreatic function.
